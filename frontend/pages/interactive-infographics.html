Follow the journey of waste from your home to proper disposal. Each step is crucial for environmental protection!

1. Segregate at Home

Separate waste into wet, dry, and hazardous categories at the source. Use different colored bins for easy identification.

2. Collection & Transport

Municipal services collect segregated waste and transport it to appropriate facilities for processing.

3. Processing & Recycling

Dry waste is sorted and sent to recycling plants. Wet waste goes to composting facilities to create nutrient-rich soil.

4. Reuse & Reduce

Recycled materials become new products. Compost enriches soil. Hazardous waste is safely disposed of, protecting our environment.
